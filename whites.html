Whites

Comtés Rhodaniens IGP

Minimal

Here is the "dry wine" version of our Sauvignon Blanc. The result is a dry white wine with an amazing profile, mixing aromas of citrus (grapefruit) and exotic fruits (passion fruit). The wine is supported by a beautiful freshness conferred in part by its beautiful acidity. Astonishing, precise and refreshing.

  • Variety: Sauvignon Blanc
  • Aging: 100% tank (stainless steel)
  • Production: 1,000 - 2,000 bottles
  • Storage: 2 - 5 years

Comtés Rhodaniens IGP

Nouvelle garde

This north Côte du Rhône grape variety shows us from year to year that it is perfectly adapted to the current Beaujolais climate. We note the varietal typical aromas with yellow fruits and white flowers but also empyreumatic notes brought by aging in barrels. The wine keeps a nice freshness.

  • Variety: Viognier
  • Aging: 50% tank (stainless steel), 50 % barrel
  • Production: 2,000 - 3,000 bottles
  • Storage: 2 - 5 years

Beaujolais Villages AOP

Chardonnay

This wine comes from the Chardonnay grape, which is the only white variety authorized within the perimeter of the appellation. Fresh and elegant, with a bouquet of pear, pineapple and also white flowers. Toasty hints complete the aromatic palette of our Beaujolais Villages Blanc. This aroma is due to a partial aging in wood, which also contributes to its body and structure.

  • Variety: Chardonnay
  • Aging: 50% tank (stainless steel), 50% barrel
  • Production: 2,000 - 3,000 bottles
  • Storage: 2 - 5 years

Vin de France

Rococo

Producing Sauvignon Blanc in Beaujolais is already original, but here it is a liquoreux moreover! We let the grapes for several months on the vines to have a raisining to obtain a maximum sugar concentration. The wine is rich and has aromas of exotic fruits (passion and mango) but remains supported by a beautiful freshness. A round and fruity pleasure.

  • Variety: Syrah
  • Aging: 100% tank (stainless steel)
  • Production: 1,500 - 2,500 bottles
  • Storage: 3 - 10 years
